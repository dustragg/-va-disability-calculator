Disclaimer: This is an educational estimate only, not legal, medical, or VA claims advice. Results are possible estimates and likely ranges based on simplified rating criteria. Actual VA decisions depend on medical evidence, exams, service connection, effective dates, pyramiding rules, analogous ratings, and adjudicator findings.

How to use this estimator

  1. Select answers that best match the evidence for each condition.
  2. Leave a condition at “not claimed / no compensable symptoms” if it should not be included.
  3. Review each rationale and the combined-rating calculation. Bilateral factor, pyramiding limits, special monthly compensation, protected ratings, and effective-date rules are not included in this version.
